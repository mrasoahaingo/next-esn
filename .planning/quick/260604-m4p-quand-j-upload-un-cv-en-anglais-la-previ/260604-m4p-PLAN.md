<objective>
Fix the PDF preview resetting to French labels when an English CV is uploaded.

Purpose: When a candidate uploads a CV in English, the extraction workflow streams partial results
back to the client. The `language` field is extracted in the identity branch (first to complete),
but `mergeExtractedPartial` silently drops it because there is no handler for `language`. As a
result, the streamed `object` always has `language: undefined`, causing the PDF template to fall
back to `'fr'` via `data.language ?? 'fr'`. The fix is a one-line addition to the merge function.

Root cause: `lib/services/extraction-merge.ts` line 74 — `language` field missing from the
`mergeExtractedPartial` function. All other ExtractedCV fields (personalInfo, summary, experiences,
education, skills, strengths) are handled; `language` and `sectionSpacing` were never added.

Output: A PDF preview that shows English section labels during streaming when the CV is in English.
</objective>
